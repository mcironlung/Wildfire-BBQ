ABOUT US

OVER 20 YEARS OF DELICIOUS BBQ!

WildFire BBQ was started over twenty years ago. In 2003, Jeff took his passion for BBQ to the next level and opened WildFire BBQ in Franklin, KY. Everyone at WildFire BBQ takes barbecue serious.

We want you, our amazing customers, to know that you are important to us. We recognize that you "chose" to eat with us and because of that, we want to say THANK YOU. No matter how large or small your order is, we appreciate your business. It is our greatest pleasure to serve you the best BBQ you've ever eaten right here in Franklin, KY.
